About Us

Just like you, we are simply fellow students who wish to share our passion for Trading Card Games. And make it more accessible for everyone.

Our Plans

- First and foremost, we intend to sell cards at market price.

- Then, we will organize time zones where we simply gather, relax, and play various card/board games.

- At the same time, we will occasionally host card games during common lunch breaks.

- And finally, we plan to organize competitions of card games.
